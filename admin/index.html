Mirage
The women you wish you knew.
What it is

AI content platform. Curated AI-generated characters called Icons. Non-explicit, clearly labeled AI. American Apparel 2000s editorial aesthetic — film grain, desaturated, natural light. PWA delivery (no app store fees).

The model

Proximity and distance. Real enough to text, intimate enough to share things with — always slightly out of reach. That tension is the product.

Tiers

Glimpse $4.99 / Illusion $14.99 / Mirage VIP $39.99

At scale (10K subscribers, 30/55/15 tier mix)

$172K gross  /  $145K net  /  84.5% margin  /  $1.74M annual
