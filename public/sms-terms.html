SMS Messaging
Terms & Opt-In

By subscribing to Mirage Icons and providing your phone number, you agree to receive text messages from Mirage Icons characters and platform notifications. Here's exactly what that means.

How You Opt In

You opt in to SMS messaging when you create a Mirage Icons account and provide your mobile phone number during the signup process. Opt-in is voluntary. You will not receive messages if you do not provide your number.

Message Types

  • Character messages from Mirage Icons (Sloan, Vera, Aria, and others)
  • Platform updates and account notifications
  • Content alerts and new release announcements

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You may receive multiple messages per week depending on your subscription tier and engagement. Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier and plan.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any message to opt out immediately. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent. You may also opt out through your account settings at mirageicons.com.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us at support@mirageicons.com.

Privacy

Your phone number and messaging data is handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy. We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes. Mirage Icons uses Twilio to deliver messages. Standard carrier rates may apply.

Supported Carriers

AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, Sprint, Boost, Cricket, MetroPCS, U.S. Cellular, and most major US carriers. Coverage may vary.
