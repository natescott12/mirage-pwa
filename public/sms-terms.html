SMS Messaging Terms and Opt-In

By subscribing to Mirage Icons and providing your phone number, you agree to receive text messages from Mirage Icons characters and platform notifications.

How You Opt In

You opt in to SMS messaging when you create a Mirage Icons account and provide your mobile phone number during signup. Opt-in is voluntary. You will not receive messages if you do not provide your number.

Message Types

  • Character messages from Mirage Icons (Sloan, Vera, Aria)
  • Platform updates and account notifications
  • Content alerts and new release announcements

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies by subscription tier. You may receive several messages per week. Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier and plan.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any message to opt out immediately. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent. You may also opt out through your account settings at mirageicons.com.

Help

Reply HELP to any message, or contact us at support@mirageicons.com.

Privacy

Your phone number is handled per our Privacy Policy. We do not sell your number to third parties. Messages are delivered via Twilio. Standard carrier rates may apply.

Supported Carriers

AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, Sprint, Boost, Cricket, MetroPCS, U.S. Cellular, and most major US carriers.
