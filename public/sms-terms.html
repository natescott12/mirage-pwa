Text Message Program

What you are signing up for

Intro LA LLC sends SMS messages to subscribers who have opted in via our website. Messages include subscription updates, notifications, and responses to customer inquiries.

Message frequency

Frequency varies by subscriber activity, typically 1 to 5 messages per week.

Charges

Message and data rates may apply.

How to opt out

Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

Help

Reply HELP for assistance. You will receive a response from Intro LA LLC. You may also contact us at hello@mirageicons.com.

Data sharing

Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.
