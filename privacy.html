Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

When you sign up for Mirage we collect your name, email address, phone number, and basic usage data — what you tap, what you read, how often you visit. That's it.

How we use it

We use this information to provide the service, send the SMS messages you've explicitly opted into, and improve the platform over time. We don't use your data to train external models or build profiles for advertisers.

Sharing

We don't sell personal data to third parties. We share it only with the infrastructure providers we rely on to run Mirage (e.g., hosting, SMS delivery, payment processing), and only to the minimum extent required for those services to work.

Security

We protect your data with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest, restrict internal access on a need-to-know basis, and keep credentials and personal information separated. No system is perfectly secure, but we treat your data the way we'd want ours treated.

Your rights

You can request a copy of your data, correct inaccuracies, or ask us to delete your account at any time. Email us and we'll handle it.
